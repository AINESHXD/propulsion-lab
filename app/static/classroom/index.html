PropulsionLab · Classroom

Design challenges

One guided problem per engine family. Set the design variables, then check your answer, every target is graded against the same cycle solver that powers the console. Green means you hit it.

These are teaching exercises with deliberately approachable targets, not a graded assessment. The numbers come from the reduced-order, educational cycle models; treat them as trend-correct, not certified design data. Want the full deck? Open the console.
